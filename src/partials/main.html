About the Game

Clearbend places players in the role
of an investigator examining unusual events across a quiet town.

By collecting clues, reviewing evidence, and reconstructing timelines, players gradually uncover the connections behind each case.

There's always more than meets the eye in each location - you just gotta know where to look.

Core Gameplay

You can't just randomly click through - you actually need to think about how everything fits together.

Explore investigation scenes

Inspect objects and environmental clues

Collect evidence fragments

Connect clues within a case board

Reconstruct event timelines
